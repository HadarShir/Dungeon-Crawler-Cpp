Dungeon Game is a text-based adventure game where the player navigates through a series of rooms, each potentially containing monsters.
The player can heal at campfires, fight monsters, and progress through a dungeon to reach the outside and win the game.

The player is represented as an entity with life and damage properties. The player can reduce damage and heal.
The dungeon consists of rooms, and the player moves between connected rooms based on available connections.
Each room may have different attributes like contain monsters to fight or healing from campfires.
In a room that contain a monster with life and damage properties. The player must fight the monster to progress.
The game ends when the player wins by escaping the dungeon or loses when their life reaches zero.
